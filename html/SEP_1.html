Project Development

Our team decided to approach this project through using a scenario to help us visualize the needs & requirements of the project. That being the Security of Changi Airport Jewel. The business needs then would be to perform indoors patrol and object detection & identification. Our risks and issues would be the supply chain (Being unable to obtain materials as required) and Hardware Reliability (Being unable to obtain accurate test results). The assumption is that the arena simulation accurately reflects the system's performance in the real-life environment.

We began in Concept stage of our project's life cycle, where we defined the problem space. In order to get a better grasp of the Changi Airport Jewel environment, we conducted exploratory research by going down to Jewel as a team and took photos of landmarks and objects of interests. We also identified the Key Stakeholders which aside from the Project Sponsor (SIT), were the users (Airport Security) and the Consumers (Tourists).

We defined the preliminary OpsCon (Operational Concept) based on our scenario. The system should require minimal interference from the Airport Security operator while being able to patrol and scan areas around the airport in order to identify objects of interest.

Defining the problem space. Identifying the Stakeholders. Defining the preliminary OpsCon. Managing Stakeholders Requirements. Identifying Stakeholders needs. Define their needs. Define system Requirements. Design definition. Integration. Verification and validation.
